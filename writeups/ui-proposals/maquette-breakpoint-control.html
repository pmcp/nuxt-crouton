Maquette — scrolling-ruler breakpoint control

The needle is fixed at center — it's the current width. Drag the ruler (left = narrower, right = wider) and the breakpoint dots scroll past at a fixed spacing. When a dot rolls under the needle it snaps and lights up — that's the breakpoint you're editing. The layout below stays centered and scales to fit.
